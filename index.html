About Me

I'm a final-year engineering student passionate about building games, apps, and AI-driven tools that solve real-world problems. I develop games using Unity and Pygame (spooky puzzles, Mario-style action), prototype social apps like Tourgram (a Splitwise + Instagram + Hinge for solo travelers), and work on full-stack MERN projects with scalable backend systems. My current research explores LLM watermarking and steganography. I enjoy coding in Python, C++, JavaScript, and working with tools like MongoDB, Flask, Unity Visual Scripting, Postman, Mongoose, 8086 Assembly, and GitHub Actions. I’m also diving deep into Competitive Programming to strengthen my algorithmic thinking. As my class’s CR, I lead coordination, management, and mentorship efforts. Self-taught through courses, documentation, and hands-on practice, I’m always eager to connect over research, game mechanics, backend design, or building cool things.
